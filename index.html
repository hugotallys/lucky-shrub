Transform Your Space Into an Oasis

Based in Tuscon, Arizona, Lucky Shrub is your one-stop shop for garden design, creation, and maintenance. We specialize in transforming any space into an oasis you can be proud of, whether it's a private backyard or a commercial property.

Expert Garden Design

Our lead architect, Jason, creates and oversees all designs. He works closely with clients to develop beautiful, functional, and sustainable landscapes, managing his team of landscapers from creation to final installation.

Landscaping & Maintenance

From new construction landscaping to routine garden care, our team handles it all. We offer comprehensive maintenance plans to ensure your space stays healthy and beautiful long after the initial creation.

Our Plant Nursery

Visit our local nursery, managed by Maria, to find a wide selection of high-quality indoor and outdoor plants. Our expert staff can help you find the perfect plants to complete your home or garden.
